The Humble Australian Meat Pie

Description

The golden standard of Australian cuisine. Can you really call yourself Australian until you have eaten a meat pie?

Luckily making this gem is real easy, and you'll be a true blue in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large brown onion, finely chopped
  • 500g Extra Lean Beef Mince
  • 1 tbsp Cornflour
  • 3/4 cup Massel beef stock
  • 3/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp barbecue sauce
  • 1 tsp Vegemite
  • 2 sheets frozen shortcrust pastry, thawed
  • 2 sheets frozen puff pastry, thawed
  • 1 egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion. Cook for 3 minutes or until soft.
    Add mince. Cook for 4 minutes, stirring with a wooden spoon, or until browned.
  2. Mix cornflour and 1 tablespoon of stock to form a paste. Add remaining stock. Add stock, sauces and Vegemite to mince.
    Bring to the boil. Reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer for 8 minutes or until thick. Cool.
  3. Preheat oven to 220Â°C. Place a baking tray into oven. Grease 4 x 8cm base measurement pie pans.
  4. Cut 4 x 15cm circles from shortcrust pastry. Use to line bases and sides of pans. Fill with mince. Brush rims with water.
    Cut 4 x 15cm circles from puff pastry. Place over meat. Press to seal. Trim. Brush with egg. Season.
  5. Place pies onto hot tray. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until golden. Serve.

Notes

Careful not to rush in and bite into this delicious pie before it cools down!
